I measured the time on 1024^2 matrix with 1024 block size, and the difference was not noticeable going from x to y in the

Ex5:
Best times:
With TILE_WIDTH = 8: 317 microseconds
With TILE_WIDTH = 16: 287 microseconds
With TILE_WIDTH = 32: 294 microseconds

No significant difference in different tile sizes.

The best time for non-tiled was 327 microseconds.

No significant difference here either.

This non-significant difference that we see, might be because small matrix size.
The problem I had was when I increased the matrix size, the resulting matrix was wrongly
computed for some odd reason. 256^2 matrices works, though, and with tile sizes of {8, 16, 32}.
